Deep Neural Networks in TMVA

Posted on April 28, 2015

In order to get familiar with TMVA and to introduce the first benchmark dataset, in this post we will use TMVA to train a deep neural network on the Higgs-Higgs dataset as described in the paper by Baldi, Sadowski and Whiteson.

The Data

The dataset consists of simulated events of two gluon into a Higgs boson. In the process (gg → H), a heavy, electrically neutral Boson is formed, which decays into a heavy, electrically charged boson H± and a W Boson. The electrically charged Higgs boson then decays into a second W boson and the light Higgs boson h0.
